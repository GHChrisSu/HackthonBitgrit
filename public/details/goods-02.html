IP refers to "Intellectual Property", literally translated as intellectual property, originally intended to be the right generated by intellectual-based creative activities.

The circulation of copyright is the process by which IP demanders (intermediaries) purchase licenses from IP owners and then authorize secondary commercial development. Consumers consume the goods made by the middlemen and complete the business closed loop.

Pain points:   In the traditional commercial authorization, taking the authorization of animated goods as an example, the time cost of one authorization is 3 months. According to the authorized use period, the authorized capital cost is 3 to 8 million yen (minimum share amount) and the reserve is +4%~ 10% project share (authorization ratio based on minimum shipments) This data is general commercial data, including T-shirts, hand-held, etc.   The basis for the authorization decision is   High and low shipments   Planning submission sooner or later   Length of cooperation history, etc.   The right to transfer property rights is isolated in the existing business environment and the circulation is slow.

solution:   Through the chain of the cloud, each product is given an independent number to ensure its regularity and scarcity. Enable digital assets to complete the transfer.

Smart contract   The proportion of the benefits generated during the circulation of goods is initially written into the smart contract, so that the participating parties can distribute the corresponding profits.

participants:   IP holder   IP broker (purchasing property rights, making & selling goods)   IP consumer

Intermediaries purchase commercialization rights from IP holders, make related goods, and sell them to consumers.
